Horizon HackerHouse

A Savannah Oasis for African Developers

Horizon Hackerhouse is your gateway to building the future. Nestled in a tranquil savannah setting, away from the city's distractions, we offer a unique co-living, co-working experience for African developers like you.

Our mission is simple: gather builders from across the continent to tackle the world's biggest challenges with cutting-edge technologies like AI and blockchain.

Join a Cohort of Changemakers

We believe innovation thrives in collaboration. Horizon Hackerhouse enrolls developers in cohorts of 10, where you'll spend two action-packed weeks coding, connecting, and thriving alongside your peers. Our program is designed to equip you with the skills and connections to make a global impact.

Horizon HackerHouse Program

Our program is divided into three phases, each named after Swahili words for different times of day, reflecting the rhythm of focus and collaboration:

Asubuhi (Dawn):
A two-week intensive introduction to the fundamentals of AI and blockchain. Gain a solid understanding of these emerging technologies and their potential to revolutionize industries.

Mchana (Midday):
Dive deep for three weeks, building solutions using Rust and Solidity. These powerful programming languages will empower you to turn your ideas into reality.
Jioni (Dusk):
The culmination of your journey. Here, you'll be placed in teams for an exciting hackathon with cash prizes. Put your newfound skills to the test and collaborate to create groundbreaking solutions.
